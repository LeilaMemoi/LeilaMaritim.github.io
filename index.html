I am a student pursuing a Masters degree in Geoinformatics, specializing in Geodata science under the Copernicus Master in Digital Earth program. I am currently studying at Paris Lodron Universitat Salzburg and will be pursuing my second year of studies at Universite Bretagne du Sud. My primary focus is on cloud computing with a special interest in geospatial applications. I am highly motivated to develop reliable and scalable data-intensive applications that will have a positive impact on society.In addition to my studies, I aspire to be an educator who makes geospatial data and services available to everyone. I believe that everyone should have access to the benefits of geospatial technology, and I am committed to making this a reality. When I'm not working on my studies, I enjoy hiking and painting. Being creative is a key part of my life, and I find it to be a great way to unwind and recharge.
